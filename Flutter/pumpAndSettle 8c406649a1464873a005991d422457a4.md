# `PatrolTester.pumpWidgeAndSettle()`

とは

In Flutter, "pumping" means rendering frames to the screen.

Pumps `widget` and then calls [WidgetTester.pumpAndSettle](https://api.flutter.dev/flutter/flutter_test/WidgetTester/pumpAndSettle.html).

This is a convenience method combining [WidgetTester.pumpWidget](https://api.flutter.dev/flutter/flutter_test/WidgetTester/pumpWidget.html) and [WidgetTester.pumpAndSettle](https://api.flutter.dev/flutter/flutter_test/WidgetTester/pumpAndSettle.html).
